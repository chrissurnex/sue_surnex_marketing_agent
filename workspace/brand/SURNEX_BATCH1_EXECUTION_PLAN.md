## Step 5 — Winner rule
Only one winner per asset family moves forward to refinement.

That means after Batch 1, we should have:

- 1 AI visibility winner
- 1 SEO + AI unified winner

## Step 6 — Revision round
Only refine winners if the issue is specific, such as:
- needs cleaner headline safe zone
- needs more product fidelity
- needs stronger focal hierarchy
- needs reduced glow/noise

Do not refine losers.

Batch 1 succeeds if we get:
