# Asset Set 1 — Homepage Hero

## 1.1 Objective
Create the primary brand/product hero image for Surnex.

The visual should communicate:
- serious modern search intelligence
- premium dark technical quality
- AI search + SEO capability
- operator-grade trust

## 1.2 Recommended Fidelity Mode
**Mode B / Mode C hybrid**
- product-led enough to feel real
- stylized enough to feel premium and campaign-worthy

## 1.3 Composition Rules
- one dominant dashboard or intelligence object
- optional one or two secondary supporting panels
- clean space for headline overlay
- strong left/right or centered focal hierarchy
- no overcrowding

## 1.4 Format Targets
- primary: 16:9
- secondary: 21:9

## 1.5 Variant A — Safer
### Intent
Cleaner, more product-led, lower visual aggression.

### Prompt
> Premium homepage hero for Surnex, realistic dark SEO and AI-search analytics dashboard in a clean tactical environment, persistent left sidebar, rounded cards, KPI summary row, line charts and donut charts, subtle lime green accent framing, high contrast black and charcoal surfaces, serious B2B SaaS mood, modern and operator-grade, clean focal hierarchy, space for headline overlay, optimized for 16:9, avoid excessive glow, toy dashboard styling, cyberpunk clutter, rainbow gradients, and fake futuristic interface elements

### What to look for
- strong credibility
- enough product realism
- no visual chaos
- headline space remains usable

## 1.6 Variant B — Balanced
### Intent
Best default direction. Product truth plus more memorable brand energy.

### Prompt
> Premium homepage hero for Surnex featuring a realistic dark analytics dashboard with a persistent left sidebar, KPI cards, trend charts, donut charts, and dense operational modules, presented in a refined tactical brand environment with controlled lime routing paths, subtle signal energy, and crisp modular depth, high contrast black and charcoal surfaces, serious and modern, built for an AI search and SEO platform for agencies, optimized for 16:9, avoid neon overload, generic AI wallpaper, glassmorphism, and fantasy interface design

### What to look for
- feels like Surnex specifically
- strong but disciplined energy
- real product cues without clutter
- good first-impression value

## 1.7 Variant C — Bolder
### Intent
More campaign-like, stronger signal energy, still within system.

### Prompt
> Bold homepage hero for Surnex, dark premium technical composition centered on a realistic Surnex-style analytics dashboard, layered with routed lime signal paths, precise node structures, atmospheric depth, and controlled motion energy, strong contrast, tactical and operator-grade, modern search intelligence aesthetic, sharp and memorable, optimized for 16:9 with clear focal hierarchy and usable copy space, avoid cheesy sci-fi effects, glowing every edge, crypto aesthetic, and fake consumer-app UI

### What to look for
- stronger memorability
- not too noisy
- product remains believable
- energy does not overpower clarity

## 1.8 Kill Conditions
Reject if:
- dashboard looks generic or fake
- too much green floods the image
- copy space disappears
- it feels like a gaming/crypto brand
- UI loses credibility

# Asset Set 2 — AI Visibility Feature Visual

## 2.1 Objective
Create the visual that explains/represents AI visibility, citations, mentions, answer presence, and prompt-level search intelligence.

The visual should communicate:
- modern search monitoring
- AI-answer presence and citation logic
- insight into visibility gaps
- serious intelligence tooling, not chatbot novelty

## 2.2 Recommended Fidelity Mode
**Mode B**
- product-inspired
- partially abstracted for conceptual clarity

## 2.3 Composition Rules
- medium complexity
- should work in feature section contexts
- can include dashboard fragments, citation nodes, answer-routing structures
- avoid literal chat bubbles dominating the image

## 2.4 Format Targets
- primary: 4:3
- secondary: 1:1
- optional: 16:9

## 2.5 Variant A — Safer
### Intent
More structured and UI-adjacent, lower abstraction.

### Prompt
> Feature visual for Surnex AI visibility, dark technical composition with realistic analytics module fragments, citation indicators, mention signals, answer presence cues, and structured dashboard cards, subtle lime highlights on black and charcoal surfaces, premium B2B SaaS aesthetic, serious and precise, optimized for 4:3, suitable for a website feature section, avoid chatbot consumer UI, robot imagery, rainbow gradients, and generic AI wallpaper

### What to look for
- clear AI visibility meaning
- feels serious and product-adjacent
- avoids generic chatbot tropes

## 2.6 Variant B — Balanced
### Intent
Best default: product logic plus more conceptual signal-routing clarity.

### Prompt
> AI visibility feature visual for Surnex, dark premium composition combining realistic analytics dashboard fragments with routed answer paths, citation nodes, mention indicators, and structured signal flows, disciplined lime green accents, black and charcoal surfaces, precise modern B2B design language, operator-grade and trustworthy, optimized for 4:3, no consumer chatbot screens, no sci-fi robot motifs, no cluttered fantasy UI

### What to look for
- good bridge between product and concept
- strong clarity of “visibility/citations/answers”
- premium, not gimmicky

## 2.7 Variant C — Bolder
### Intent
More expressive motion and signal energy while staying technical.

### Prompt
> Bold AI visibility visual for Surnex, dark tactical environment showing routed answer signals, connected citation nodes, intelligent visibility paths, and selective analytics interface fragments, disciplined lime accents with controlled motion energy, premium operator-grade search intelligence aesthetic, high contrast, optimized for 4:3, avoid glowing sci-fi chaos, abstract nonsense, chatbot bubbles everywhere, and decorative visual noise

### What to look for
- strong category distinctiveness
- still readable as a feature visual
- energy supports meaning, not clutter

## 2.8 Kill Conditions
Reject if:
- it looks like a chatbot app
- it looks like generic “AI brain” art
- product fragments look fake or toy-like
- visibility/citation concept is unclear

# Asset Set 3 — SEO + AI Search Unified Visual

## 3.1 Objective
Create the visual that explains Surnex’s category wedge: classic SEO intelligence and AI-search visibility in one operating layer.

The visual should communicate:
- one system, not fragmented tools
- rankings/backlinks/audits + AI visibility/citations together
- modern search stack unification

## 3.2 Recommended Fidelity Mode
**Mode B hybrid**
- strategic and conceptual
- grounded in real analytics forms

## 3.3 Composition Rules
- unified system feel
- not a cheesy “left side vs right side” split unless done with discipline
- can use a routing spine, central core, or layered intelligence architecture
- should imply integration, not juxtaposition only

## 3.4 Format Targets
- primary: 16:9
- secondary: 4:5

## 3.5 Variant A — Safer
### Intent
More straightforward, dashboard-centric, clarity first.

### Prompt
> Unified search intelligence visual for Surnex, realistic dark analytics environment combining SEO dashboard elements such as rankings, backlinks, audit metrics, and trend charts with AI visibility cues such as answer presence, citations, and routed mention signals, all within one cohesive system, black and charcoal surfaces with disciplined lime accents, premium and credible B2B SaaS style, optimized for 16:9, avoid cheesy split-screen gimmicks, excessive glow, and fake futuristic clutter

### What to look for
- clarity of unification
- realistic product-adjacent feel
- no awkward visual divide

## 3.6 Variant B — Balanced
### Intent
Best default direction: unified operating layer with stronger visual concept.

### Prompt
> Unified search operating layer visual for Surnex, dark tactical composition connecting traditional SEO intelligence and AI-search visibility within one premium system, realistic dashboard structures, backlink and ranking cues, audit and analytics modules, routed answer paths and citation indicators, all linked through a clear signal spine with disciplined lime accents, operator-grade, modern, high contrast, optimized for 16:9, avoid gimmicky split-screen design, generic AI art, and cluttered sci-fi effects

### What to look for
- strong “one platform” message
- strategic clarity
- distinctive enough for category positioning

## 3.7 Variant C — Bolder
### Intent
More concept-led and campaign-worthy, but still credible.

### Prompt
> Bold unified search intelligence visual for Surnex, premium dark technical environment showing SEO analytics modules and AI-search visibility systems converging through routed lime signal architecture, layered dashboard fragments, structured depth, precise node and path design, serious and operator-grade, memorable and strategic, optimized for 16:9, avoid cheesy symbolism, neon overload, and fake speculative UI

### What to look for
- memorable category image
- not too abstract
- integration remains obvious
- still feels premium and credible

## 3.8 Kill Conditions
Reject if:
- the piece feels patched together
- SEO and AI sides feel unrelated
- visual uses cliché split-screen tropes badly
- it becomes abstract enough to lose the message

## 4. Output Review Sheet

Use this after each batch.

### Score 1–5
- Brand fit
- Product truth
- Message clarity
- Visual impact
- Reusability
- Thumbnail strength

### Keep if
- brand fit is 4+
- message clarity is 4+
- product truth is 3+ for product-led assets

### Refine if
- strong direction but cluttered
- good composition but weak product cues
- good fidelity but too safe/forgettable

### Kill if
- generic AI startup look
- crypto/cyberpunk drift
- fake dashboard nonsense
- too much lime
- no usable hierarchy
